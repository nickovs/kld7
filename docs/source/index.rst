A driver for the K-LD7 radar module
===================================

The K-LD7 is a small and low cost digital 24GHz radar motion detector
made by RFBeam in Switzerland. It can detect and track multiple
targets and provide distance, horizontal angle, speed and magnitude
information for each target as well as filtered tracking for the most
significant target.
